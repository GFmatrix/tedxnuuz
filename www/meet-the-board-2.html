MEET THE BOARD

MEET THE BOARD

  • Melina Zarboulas

    PRESIDENT

    Melina Zarboulas is the president of TEDxNUUZ, and a sophomore studying information and computer science with a focus on textile design. She began watching TED talks when she was younger at the request of her acting instructor, who valued the immense presentation talent and impact the speakers held throughout their talks. Since then, she has found herself captivated by so many different areas of interest, and has loved how TED talks enhance dynamics of speech and education through the power of presenting. What Melina loves most about TEDx talks is their ability to bring together people from all backgrounds, careers, and experiences, and how these people can all seamlessly intertwine their stories to paint a larger picture. As the lead organizer of the 2023 TEDxNUUZ event, she hopes that the audience can absorb information on many different subject cores through the admirable speakers and their talks.

  • Tula Cox

    VICE PRESIDENT

    Tula Cox is the Vice President of TEDxNUUZ. She is a sophomore studying animal science with hopes to be a veterinarian. One thing about TED that Tula has always appreciated is the diversity of talks and speakers. She finds that the speakers are all so different and have so many unique experiences to share. Tula loves that the speakers all tie together to the theme and makes the experience so impactful to the audience. As a young adult, Tula really values how a lot of the talks help her stay educated on a lot of different current situations in society and herself. As an organizer of the event, she hopes that her peers and generation can navigate their experiences after learning of some lasting effects of the COVID-19 pandemic on their lives.
