Sustainable Growth

October 15th, 2023

National University of Uzbekistan, Ziyo forum
9:00 AM

Hey there, young changemakers! Are you a student with a burning passion to save the planet? Do you have an innovative idea that could help combat climate change? Well, we've got some exciting news for you! We're organizing a TEDx NUUz countdown event in October, focused on climate change, and we're looking for passionate students like you to share their ideas and solutions. Whether you're an environmental science whiz, a social justice advocate, or simply someone with a bright idea, we want to hear from you! As a speaker at our event, you'll have the opportunity to share your ideas, connect with eco activists, and inspire others to join the fight against the biggest challenges humanity has ever faced. Our mission is to spread ideas, inspire action, and foster a community of curious and courageous thinkers. Let's make the world safer and greener together! Join us!
